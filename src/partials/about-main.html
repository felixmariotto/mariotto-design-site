Mariotto Design was created by Felix Mariotto, who previously worked as computer-aided-designer for high jewelry workshops in France and Hong Kong. The company is decentralized and rely on a network of partners scattered accross Europe.

Whatever your scale and workflow, Mariotto Design can adapt and fit in your pipeline. We can offer our services in English, French and Italian.
